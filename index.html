About Me

Hi there! My name is Thuan.
I'm Vietnamese, a total computer geek, and a person who is passionate about playing and creating 3-D video games. I speak Vietnamese, English, and a little bit of Korean. I'm currently a student majoring in Computer Engineering at University of Information Technology.
